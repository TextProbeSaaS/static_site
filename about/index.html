Our Mission

TextProbe is a SaaS platform designed to assist marketers and businesses in gaining deeper insights into their customers through advanced text mining capabilities. Our goal is to empower organizations of all sizes to harness the power of cutting-edge tools for natural language processing (NLP) and natural language understanding (NLU) without the need for extensive in-house expertise. We believe in democratizing AI-driven text analysis to foster innovation and drive growth for every business.

TextProbe Platform

  • Text Classification: TextProbe’s text classification feature allows users to categorize and classify text data effortlessly, even without pre-defined labels or extensive training datasets. Our AI models can generalize from minimal examples, thus saving valuable time and resources.
  • Collection and Processing: With integrated email and SMS capabilities, TextProbe enables dynamic App to Person interactions, facilitating seamless data export and response management.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Our platform boasts an intuitive interface, ensuring that users of all technical backgrounds can easily interact with and derive insights from textual data tailored to their specific industry.
  • Integrated with Leading AI Models: By utilizing your API key, you can leverage top AGI models from OpenAI and Claude within our platform, enhancing the capabilities of our tools and maximizing your results.
  • Robust Support: Our dedicated support team is committed to assisting users at every step of their TextProbe journey, offering comprehensive onboarding assistance, troubleshooting support, and ensuring success with our platform.

How TextProbe Works

TextProbe simplifies NLP, NLU, and text mining processes. Whether by uploading text data or conducting SMS or email surveys, our platform’s text classification and summarization capabilities enable effortless comprehension of client conversations, empowering businesses to gain valuable insights efficiently.
